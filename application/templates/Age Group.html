What is the age-group that is most likely affected by gaming anxiety?
Did you know?
Age is divided into five sub-groups: 18 to 25, 26 to 33, 34 to 40, 41 to 48, and 49 to 56. While gamer anxiety does not appear to vary much among the first four subgroups, the final group composing of the oldest individuals (49-56) are known to bear significantly lower anxiety levels than that of younger individuals. This is surprising considering studies have shown that anxiety in general becomes more common with old age, and is more commonly found in middle aged-adults (36 to 55). This can be attributed to individuals experiencing more stress-inducing events at this age (Guarnotta, 2020). Thus, it can be inferred that the gaming anxiety experienced by individuals aged 49 to 56 is relatively low, as videogames serve as their temporary getaway from these stressful events.
