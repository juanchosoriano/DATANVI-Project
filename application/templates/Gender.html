Does the gender of the individual gamer affect gaming anxiety?
Did you know?
Gender is divided among three sub-groups: males, females and other. According to the data, females and those identifying as genders classified under the "other" sub-group are those who are more likely to experience gaming anxiety. This can be due to them being treated differently than their male peers when gaming. Female gamers are more often to report receiving negative treatment such as harassment, and toxic behavior than males (McLean & Griffiths, 2019) According to the World Health Organization (WHO), they are also more likely to be diagnosed with anxiety disorders than that of men.
